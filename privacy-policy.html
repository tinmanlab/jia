Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-03

1. Data controller and scope

Seeds of Seeing uses only client-side rendering for generation workflows. Any inputs you type on the page (seed, iteration count, UI preferences, API keys) remain in your browser local storage unless you export session data.

This policy applies to the website content served at aro-vision.vercel.app.

2. Advertising data

This site uses Google AdSense to show contextual ads. Google may use cookies and ad identifiers to measure ad performance, frequency capping, and fraud prevention. Data processing for advertising is handled by Google and subject to Google Privacy Policy.

3. Local key storage

API keys entered in the app are stored only in your browser localStorage and are sent directly from your browser to the selected providers when you run the experience. Keys are not intentionally sent to this repository or to a separate backend.

4. Cookies and analytics

We do not run separate website analytics for this site. Third parties (such as Google AdSense) may set their own cookies under their policy.

5. Changes

If the policy changes, we will update this page with the new effective date.
